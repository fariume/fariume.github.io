About Fari


Here lies the story of Fari, an only daughter.


So, who is she?

I wanna be beautiful as the ocean, because ocean is strong and feminine - and that's

what makes it ocean, as ocean.


I go by Fari, full name Farina Yasmin binti Badrul Hisham. My birthplace is from Klang, Selangor,

but my vision and heart stays in Sungai Buloh. I am the only daughter, second child of all, the one who

distributes the feminine yet the strongest of the family. My age is 21 years old, yet my mind stays as an

old 18 years old me.



What's she like?

She's very much... introvert.

Her personality just like every introvert you see, except she's more to Rue from Euphoria tv show.

Despite being most expensive child clinically, she still want to make her parents proud.

Instead of that, she still fly as a bird, trying to achieve her very best achievements.

Still, her dreams to be a writer, journalist, content creator never dies, her spirit never gave up.

Whoever she is, what she identifies as, she spread her wings just like other human being, treating kindness one and another.
